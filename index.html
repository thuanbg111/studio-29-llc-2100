Studio 29, LLC is a U.S. U.S. Limited Liability Company (LLC) charity (Business ID 001712100) delivering practical relief, workforce opportunities, and land activation with public partners.

Direct Relief

Outreach, food & hygiene, and navigation to services with trusted providers. We publish scopes and outcomes for each initiative.

Workforce
